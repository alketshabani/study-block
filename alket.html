Personal Details

My career as a System Administrator started in a intership at Raiffeisen Bank Albania. I was working in Core and Middleware team and our main focus as a team was Linux operating system and RHEL.

My path changed when i joined Deloitte Albania and Kosovo as a Dynamics AX System Administrator. At my new role i had the opportunity to work with various Microsoft technologies such as Windows Server, Hyper-V and MSSQL Database.

My career continued at Smartwork where i was IT Administrator for the whole company (about 100 people at my time, now 150). At Smartwork the technologies that i worked on include: Fortinet, Dynamics NAV, Zabbix monitoring, CentOS and Dynamics AX.
